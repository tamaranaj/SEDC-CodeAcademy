La douce maison

Bienvenue! Nous avons hâte de vous voir déguster nos délices pâtissiers!

If there is one bakery in Paris that you should make it your mission to visit above all others, make it La douce Maison, a brand which can be found at several locations dotted across the city. After all, this is probably one of the most famous bakeries in Paris.

Just a short walk away from Square Jules-Ferry (the chic waterway that dominates the 10th district of the city), Du Pain et des Idées is so successful that it doesn’t even open on weekends! Indeed, it has to be said that this boulangerie serves up some of the best pastries in Paris…

Be sure to visit earlier in the day if you want your pick of the best sweets and baked goods. When things sell out, they’re typically gone for the day so be sure to head to the store earlier on in the day in order to avoid disappointment!

For an extra special treat, consider taking your purchase to go so that you can sit by the water of Canal Saint Martin, just like the locals do! The canal in the 10th arrondissement is a hip and trendy spot where you can sit by the water, check out street art, and explore cool and unique shops.
